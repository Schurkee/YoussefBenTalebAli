About Me

I'm an aspiring game Developer from Tunisia, and I've been a gamer since I was 5. As my love for gaming grew over the years, I decided to pursue a career as a game developer and share my creativity with the world in the hopes of influencing others, especially in my home country, where gaming is underrepresented.

I'm primarily self-taught, almost everything I know about game development is from searching the internet and experimenting!

I find the combination of being a perfectionist and working well under pressure to be the perfect tool for problem-solving. It has enabled me to produce excellent outcomes in both personal projects and throughout my academic career.
